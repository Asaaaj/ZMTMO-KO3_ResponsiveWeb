New from the Apple – iPhone 11. Visual changes brings mainly a view of the back side of the phone. The Apple logo moved to the center of the phone and the cutout for the dual camera was enlarged. With this dual camera system you will take 4K videos, portraits and ultrawide photos. Because of the night mode of the camera you will take brilliant photos even in the poor light and you will not miss any of your night advetures. You will definitely enjoy 6.1“ Liquid Retina display when sliding in your gallery and remembering good old memories. The performance of the phone is taken care by a new chip developed by Apple -A13 Bionic. With Face ID there will be never a problem with unlocking your phone whenever. Battery life has significantly increased compared to the previous generation.
